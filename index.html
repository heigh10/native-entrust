OUR VISION is a future where Native communities have clarity, control, and opportunity across the lands and assets they steward.

OUR MISSION is to modernize Native estates, probates, and trust asset management through secure technology built for sovereignty and transparency.

The need

The probate system for Native American federal trust properties is overdue for a new operating model.

Communities are paying the price

  • Years-long probate delays
  • Fractionated land ownership with hundreds of heirs
  • Outdated, paper-based systems
  • Limited transparency for families
  • Billions in locked economic value

Result

Land sits idle. Families wait. Opportunity is lost.

The solution

A complete AI-powered probate and trust asset platform.

NATIVE ENTRUST transforms probate from a multi-year burden into a clearer, more transparent process.

Automated Document Processing

Extracts and organizes data from wills, death certificates, land records, and tribal rolls.

AI Heirship Mapping

Identifies rightful heirs, reconstructs lineage, and supports incomplete historical records.

Workflow Automation

Prioritizes cases, generates filings and notices, and keeps cases moving forward.

Transparency Dashboards

Gives teams real-time milestones, document access, and communication history.

Land Fractionation Modeling

Visualizes ownership complexity and identifies consolidation opportunities.

Estate Planning Tools

Supports culturally aware workflows, will creation, and future multi-language access.

What changes

Clearer cases, stronger land control, and more usable assets.

40–60% faster probate resolution
30–50% faster identification of lease-ready parcels
25–40% reduction in manual case preparation tasks
50–70% fewer routine family status inquiries
2–3x faster identification of land consolidation opportunities
